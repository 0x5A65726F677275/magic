Understand the Rules & Guidelines Before Using Our Services

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Magic Castle Production ("us", "we", "our") collects, uses, and discloses personal information about our clients, partners, and visitors who interact with our services. This policy applies to our website and services including architectural planning support, construction production, site coordination, and project communications.

By contacting us, inquiring about our services, or using our website, you consent to the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or engage with our services, we may collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Company or project information
  • Details about your creative or business needs
  • Any other information you choose to share when inquiring about our construction planning, site coordination, and advertising services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • Communicate with you about projects, plans, and collaborations
  • Send relevant information about our services, events, and opportunities
  • Improve our services and website

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only when necessary to provide our services (e.g., with partners involved in a project) or when required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.
