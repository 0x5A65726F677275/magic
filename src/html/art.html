ART

Magic Castle Production aspires to center artists' voices, provide resources, and support environments where all artists can prosper. We aspire to an artist residency field that shapes a world where people have the freedom to unleash their creative potential and prosper in inclusive, just, joyful and accessible environments.

What We Offer

Events for Artists

We center artists' voices and provide resources and support environments where all artists can prosper.

Support

We support people who work in the artist residency field. We provide training and networking opportunities; leverage funding and promote evaluation, reflection, transparency and the development of equitable practices.

Field Conversations

Discussions featuring thought leaders who address topical issues. These conversations center artists' voices and engage residency leaders to explore growth and innovation.

Learning

We listen, learn and share. We promote knowledge-sharing through research, evaluation, and information to champion the issues most relevant to the art field.

Grow Your Vision

Showcase your talents with our exclusive range of premium services. It is a site for artists, arts administrators and other creatives to find opportunities.
