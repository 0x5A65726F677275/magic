Music

MUSIC

We are a community of entrepreneurs committed to creativity and innovation. We identify and develop recording artists and songwriters, and we produce, distribute and promote the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful music to delight and entertain fans around the world.

Knowing that music, a powerful force for good in the world, is unique in its ability to inspire people and bring them together, we work with our artists and employees to serve our communities.

The way you present your music is that the world will know who you are. With our key slogan being "beautiful" what people will see is the mastery of artwork behind you, which will draw customers after customers looking for your music. We invest in our artists at every stage of their careers and strive to provide the most creative and commercial opportunities possible. We also provide our artists with the latest technology to keep track of the performances of their music, both financially and from a marketing perspective.

How We Make Magic Happen?

01

Consultation

To get the ball rolling, we'll send over an initial proposal based on everything we discussed. Once you are happy with the proposal, you sign the agreement, and we get everything booked for you. We assign you your event coordinator, who will be looking after you until the date of your event.

02

Proposal

Depending on the package you have booked, we'll invite you to join a creative video call with the band leader to discuss creative details like song choices, how the sets should be configured and any special creative details.

03

Planning & Support

Depending on the package you have booked, you have a video call with the video and lighting consultant to help you get the best look and sound for your construction. You can also discuss other aspects of your day, for example, audio services for speeches, or other AV requirements.

04

Arrangements

We develop song arrangements and live track production so you get a performance unlike any other.
